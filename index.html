About

Learn more about the journey of Eca-verse Media and our commitment to excelence.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Eca-verse Media started as a small design studio with a passion for creativity and innovation. Focused on delivering high quality Graphic designs and Branding solutions.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    Dedication to Excellence led to Rapid Growth, and Eca-verse Media expanded into a full service Agency, offering a wide range of creative services.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    We broadened our scope into Framing Services providing our clients with a complete package for their visual and branding needs.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Eca verse continued to evolve, incorprating the latest design trends and technologies to offer cutting-edge solutions to our clients.
